Question Answered (QA)

What is a pet animal?

A pet animal is a domesticated animal kept for companionship, such as dogs, cats, or rabbits.

Why do people keep pets?

People keep pets for companionship, emotional support, and sometimes for security or assistance.

How should you take care of a pet?

Provide proper food, clean water, regular vet check-ups, exercise, and love.
